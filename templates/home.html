Conspiracy Detector

Applying the marvels of modern technology to detecting clinical paranoia

What does this do?

Conspiracy Detector uses the marvels of miracles of modern mathematics and computer science to solve the prickly problem of detecting people who are totally off their rocker. In application, this means using a Binary Support Vector Machine to classify the input text based off of linguistic indicators.
